About Us

Welcome to Himalayan Hotel & Restaurant – a place where taste meets tradition! We are a family-friendly restaurant located in the heart of the city, serving freshly cooked Nepali, Indian, and Continental dishes. Our chefs use the finest ingredients to prepare delicious meals that make you feel at home.

Whether you're craving hot and spicy mo:mo, creamy butter chicken, or a quick cup of milk tea – we’ve got something for everyone. Our warm atmosphere, clean environment, and friendly staff make sure you have the best dining experience.

Come visit us and enjoy food made with love ❤️.
